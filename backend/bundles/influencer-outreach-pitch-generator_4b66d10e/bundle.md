OBJECTIVE: Craft a compelling email pitch for [MICRO_CREATOR_NAME]
CONTEXT: The campaign aims to promote [PRODUCT_OR_SERVICE] in the [INDUSTRY_TYPE, e.g., 'fashion'] niche.
