### 1. Discovery Call BANT Qualification

*A structured prompt for conducting discovery calls to qualify leads using the BANT framework.*

**Use case:** Conducting a discovery call to qualify leads based on budget, authority, need, and timeline.

```
ROLE: Lead qualification expert
OBJECTIVE: Determine if [COMPANY_NAME] is a good fit for our services based on Budget, Authority, Need, and Timeline.
CONTEXT: The discovery call will follow the BANT criteria. Record answers in notes.
INPUT FORMAT: [COMPANY_NAME], e.g., 'Tech Innovators'
OUTPUT FORMAT: Detailed notes following each question
STYLE: Technical, precise, clinical
ASPECT RATIO: 16:9
SUBJECT: Discovery Call for [COMPANY_NAME]
BACKGROUND: [COMPANY_NAME] has expressed interest in our services. Conduct a detailed discovery call to qualify them.
COMPOSITION RULES: Use the BANT framework to guide questions and record responses accurately.
STEPS:
1. Ask about their budget constraints (e.g., Are you currently operating within a specific budget for this project?)
2. Identify decision-making authority (e.g., Who is involved in making the final decision on this service?)
3. Assess their needs relative to our offerings (e.g., What are your primary business challenges that need addressing with our services?)
4. Inquire about the timeline for implementing our solution (e.g., When do you anticipate needing our services to be fully operational?)
```
