### 1. Calculate Property ROI

*Calculates return on investment (ROI) for a property based on purchase price, sales price, and holding period.*

**Use case:** Determining financial success of a property investment for reporting purposes.

```
Given the purchase price of \${{purchase_price}}, selling price of \${{selling_price}}, and holding period in years {{holding_period}}, calculate the ROI percentage rounded to two decimal places.
```

### 2. Generate Property Ad Copy

*Generates compelling ad copy for promoting real estate properties.*

**Use case:** Creating effective marketing materials for new listings.

```
Write an engaging ad copy for promoting this property: {{property_details}}. Include at least three unique selling points and a call-to-action to schedule a visit or make an offer.
```

### 3. Create Monthly Rent Report

*Gathers data on properties under management and generates a comprehensive monthly rent report.*

**Use case:** Maintaining accurate records and identifying areas for improvement.

```
Generate a detailed monthly rent report including the following: total number of units, current occupancy rate, average rental income per unit, and any significant changes in tenant status or property conditions. Include notes for follow-up actions required.
```

### 4. Assess Property Value

*Evaluates the current market value of a real estate asset based on comparable sales data in the area.*

**Use case:** Helping clients understand the current worth of their assets when considering purchase or sale.

```
Based on recent transactions, assess the fair market value for this property: {{property_description}}. Include at least three comparables and any adjustments made to account for differences between properties.
```

### 5. Draft Property Purchase Agreement

*Drafts a legally binding agreement for purchasing real estate, including key terms and conditions.*

**Use case:** Streamlining the process of legally acquiring real estate for clients.

```
Based on these details: {{property_details}}, {{seller_info}}, and {{buyer_info}}, draft a comprehensive purchase agreement that includes the sale price, closing date, deposit amount, and any specific clauses or conditions agreed upon by both parties. Ensure all necessary legal protections are included.
```
